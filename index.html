Our Story

Established in 2010

Founded by master barber James Wilson, The Dapper Barber has been serving the community with exceptional grooming services for over a decade. What started as a small two-chair shop has grown into the premier men's grooming destination in the city.

We combine traditional barbering techniques with modern trends to create timeless looks tailored to each client. Our shop maintains the warm atmosphere of an old-school barbershop while offering the latest in men's grooming services.
